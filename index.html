About Me

I'm a Senior Data Engineer at Adobe, working on the Campaign B2B data platform. I help design the data models and scoring logic that identify Marketing Qualified Accounts for upsell and cross-sell — turning noisy activity signals into trusted, actionable tables.

Over the last 14+ years, I've shipped data platforms across retail, telecom, and healthcare — previously with Latent-View Analytics (Adobe engagement), Brillio (Verizon), Cognizant (Kaiser Permanente), and Wipro (Ross Stores). My day-to-day stack is Python, SQL, Apache Spark, Airflow, Databricks, and Microsoft Azure, with deep roots in the Hadoop ecosystem from earlier projects.

Outside of my day job, I love building small side projects — scripts, mini pipelines, weekend experiments — to learn new tools and stay curious. This site is where I share them, along with notes on what I've learned along the way.

Hadoop → Databricks Migration

Led a 6-engineer team through a phased migration of 100+ legacy scripts and 400+ tables from on-prem Hadoop to Azure Databricks, with Delta Lake as the new storage layer. Rewrote Hive jobs in PySpark and tuned them for the new runtime.

Azure Databricks Delta Lake PySpark Airflow

Cloud Cost Optimization

Rolled out a post-migration optimization program on Databricks — right-sizing clusters, enforcing job clusters with auto-termination, switching compression from Snappy to gZip, rationalizing cold data, and tuning SQL to reduce shuffle and storage.
